\section*{Author Contributions}

The project is organised into four loosely-coupled modules connected by a shared
batch/token contract, which let the authors work in parallel and integrate
through reviewed pull requests.
\begin{itemize}
    \item \textbf{MENG-XI LIN} --- \emph{Data}: the synthetic score generator, the
    \texttt{music21}/\texttt{verovio} renderer, the closed-set four-stream
    vocabularies, and the multi-encoder data loaders.
    \item \textbf{CHUN-JUI HSU} --- \emph{Model}: the Vision-Encoder-Decoder
    architecture (ViT/ResNet encoders, the multi-head decoder, the CRNN+CTC
    variant), the per-head losses, and the training loop, including the
    architecture, sample-efficiency, and photo fine-tune experiments.
    \item \textbf{CHIEN-AN CHOU} --- \emph{Post-processing}: the deterministic
    mapping from the four decoupled token streams to formatted Jianpu output.
    \item \textbf{BEN-LU CHEN} --- \emph{Deployment \& integration}: the
    end-to-end inference pipeline, the command-line tool, the interactive web
    demo, and overall cross-module integration and coordination.
\end{itemize}
